Tutorial complete!

Congratulations! Now you know how to add transactions and contacts to your dashboard. Keep adding and contactging transactions and your dashboard will be organized in no time!

Here are some more tips to get you started:

  • Click on a transaction or contact to view more details about it.
  • You can edit the item you are viewing by clicking the Edit button in the Options menu.
  • Similarly, you can delete an item by clicking the Delete button in the Options menu. This action is immediate and permanent, so make sure you're absolutely certain.
  • Use setlists to create a list of transactions to plan and share future performances.
  • Customize your dashboard by clicking on Settings in the Options menu.
  • Invite members to view and manage your dashboard with you by clicking on Members in the Options menu from the dashboard.
  • You can quickly search for a transaction by keyword using the quick search at the top of the screen at any time.
  • Use Advanced Search to search for transactions based on keywords, exclude keywords, contacts, and last performed date by clicking the Advanced Search button in the Options menu of the dashboard or clicking the link in the quick search results page.

That's it! If you have any questions, don't hesitate to email support@sheetmusicorganizer.com for assistance.
